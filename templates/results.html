How the Algorithm Works
Step 1: Calculate Net Balances

For each person, we calculate: (money they're owed) - (money they owe) = net balance

Step 2: Graph Theory Model

Model debts as a directed graph where people are nodes and debts are weighted edges

Step 3: Min-Heap Optimization

Use priority queues to always match the person who owes most with the person owed most

Step 4: Greedy Settlement

Greedily settle maximum possible amounts to minimize total transaction count
